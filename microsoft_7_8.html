Microsoft posts $3.2 billion loss as it writes down Nokia phone business
Microsoft Corp (MSFT.O) reported a $3.2 billion quarterly net loss as it wrote down its Nokia phone business and demand fell for its Windows operating system.
Microsoft's shares fell 3 percent to $45.80 in extended trading on Tuesday.
