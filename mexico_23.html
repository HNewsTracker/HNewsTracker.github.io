T-Mobile US calling plans to cover Canada and Mexico
T-Mobile is now offering its U.S. customers the ability to talk, text and browse the Internet in Canada and Mexico without paying extra charges.
The company points to the large amount of travel done by its U.S. customers in Canada and Mexico, saying that wireless carriers rake in billions of dollars in roaming charges as a result.
T-Mobile US Inc. and other wireless phones do work in Canada in Mexico, but carriers often tack on hefty roaming charges.
12015-07-09 23:07:33siteT-Mobile US Inc. said this will mean savings for customers who travel to Canada or Mexico or call people there frequently.

"We're just making Canada and Mexico part of your home territory," says T-Mobile CEO John Legere said on a conference call.
Previously, T-Mobile charged 20 cents per minute for calls made while traveling in Canada and Mexico.
For calls from the U.S. to those countries, T-Mobile customers previously had to buy an add-on global calling plan or pay 49 cents per minute to call Canada and 69 cents to call Mexico.
For instance, AT&T Inc. sells packages of minutes good for calls made while traveling in Mexico and Canada.
