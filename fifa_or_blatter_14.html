A FIFA panel aiming to improve Israeli-Palestinian football relations met for the first time Wednesday and agreed a follow-up session in the Middle East next month.
Panel chairman Tokyo Sexwale, a South African businessman, hosted the two football federations' leaders, FIFA said in a statement.
On Wednesday, FIFA published a photograph of Blatter and Sexwale shaking hands with the two football federation presidents.
FIFA said the panel planned to meet in the Middle East region in September.
