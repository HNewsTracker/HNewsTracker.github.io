Ecuador's Cotopaxi volcano spews ash south of Quito
A volcano has spewed ash over a wide area in two pre-dawn blasts near Ecuador's capital.
12015-08-14 16:58:30siteThe volcano is 30 miles (50 kilometers) from Quito.
22015-08-16 02:26:06site
President Rafael Correa declared a state of emergency Saturday over increasing activity in the Cotopaxi volcano near the capital of Quito, and officials evacuated a few hundred people as a precaution.
Presidential legal secretary Alexis Mera said further ash explosions and some pyroclastic flows on the volcano's western slopes Saturday led officials to evacuate some nearby villages.
Four blasts inside the volcano sent ash shooting more than two miles in the sky Friday, coating highways, homes and cars just south of Ecuador's capital with a fine gray powder.
