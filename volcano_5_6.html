A helicopter flies over as lava erupts from the Piton de la Fournaise "Peak of the Furnace" volcano, on the southeastern corner of the Indian Ocean island of Reunion Saturday, Aug. 1, 2015.
The Piton de la Fournaise volcano - French for Peak of the Furnace - is more than 530,000 years old and is one of the world's most active, with more than 150 eruptions recorded since the 1600s.
